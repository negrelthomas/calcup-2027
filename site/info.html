Game rules

  • Playing time: 2 × 25 minutes, 5-minute half-time.
  • Played to current IHF rules. No video review, even if streamed.
  • One team time-out per team per half (1 min).
  • Ranking: points → head-to-head → goal difference → goals scored.
  • Placement ties: 7-meter shootout. Final ties: overtime, then shootout.

Code of conduct

Strictly no glue. Any violation forfeits 100% of the sportsmanship bond.
No smoking or alcohol anywhere on school premises (parking lot, venue, access road).

A $500 sportsmanship bond per team is held in escrow and returned absent disqualifications, glue use, or conduct breaches. A blue card means at least one game suspension and full bond forfeiture.

On-site services

Concession: snacks and drinks available during peak hours — price list posted at the stand.

Physiotherapy / medical: treatment area on site. Concussion protocol (SCAT6) is enforced — safety always overrides competition.
